Our Purpose

Shehacks is a great opportunity that we came across. We believe in empowering women and breaking the barriers set by the society. Shehacks, for us is a great platform for learning and building our skills. We chose shehacks as it will strengthen our skills and also to find new areas of interest.

Our Expectations

To create a well structured program for enhancing our skills and developing new ones with enthusiasm. Uplifting women in the field of engineering and helping them contribute to different realms of life. A stronger woman to woman bond to build new ideas and thus improve the standard of living.
